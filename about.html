About Me

A Tropical favourite. the Tropical Treats Bakery is the one-stop-shop for delectable yummies, refreshing drinks and smiling staff. Catch up with your friends and family for a "fresh and fantastic" meal. Babinda Bakery is at great location near Willows Shopping Centre. I am an artisan-baker and I trained in Paris. Some of my current customers are James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.
