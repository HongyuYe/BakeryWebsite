Goals: The main goal for this website is to advertise the bakery Tropical Treats and provide an online interface where potential customers can interact with the website to gain knowledge but most importantly, to increase sales at the bakery. The Goal here is to gain at least a 20% revenue base on what Tropical Treats already has in their monthly revenue.

Success Evaluation: To evaluate my success in this scenario would be easy. To have a 20% increase of the sales in the bakery Tropical Treats within 3 months. A physical number that can be monitored is always an easy measurement of success. Should we surpass the goals set by our client, the project will be successful.

Target Audience: When building the Tropical Treats website, I will need to keep in mind that they are trying to encourage a younger families and people range from 18 to 35 than what they have currently. Make the website fashionable in the design is a good way to attract a younger demographic. We need to use more pictures instead of text. We will use vibrant colors and vigorous Sign Up targeting to call our viewers into signing up. We may post some deals on the homepage and an menu on a separate page. All these things together will interest new customers.
